Automatically Enumerate Local Cameras

All the GigE Vision cameras in the same local subnet with the Software will be automatically enumerated in the Camera List.

The Software refreshes enumerated cameras on the same local subnet with the PC where the Software runs every 30 seconds.

Click to open the Camera List, and then click to refresh the enumerated cameras manually.

When the cameras are enumerated, you can double-click the camera or click to connect it to the Software if the camera status is available.

Note

For details about the status of the GigE Vision cameras, see Status of GigE Vision Camera.
